// Frequently Asked //

Questions you're
probably already thinking.

I have no skills or experience outside UPSC prep. Can eXSARC still help me?
Yes — and this is exactly who eXSARC is built for. UPSC preparation develops real, marketable skills — research, structured writing, analytical thinking, and discipline. The gap is not skills. The gap is knowing how to present and monetize them.
How soon can I realistically start earning?
Most students who follow the 21-day program consistently earn their first ₹1,000–₹5,000 within 2–3 weeks. Reaching ₹15,000–₹25,000/month typically takes 60–90 days of consistent execution.
What if I'm still considering another UPSC attempt?
You can do both. Building an income stream doesn't mean giving up on your goal — it means you're not financially dependent on the outcome. Many students continue preparing while building parallel income.
Is this only for UPSC — what about SSC, banking, or state exams?
Currently eXSARC is focused specifically on UPSC aspirants — the content, coaching and community is tailored to that journey. Adjacent exam aspirants are welcome and the principles apply broadly.
What's included in the ₹499 Career Clarity Call?
A 30-minute focused session covering: skill audit, 2–3 career path options, 30-day action plan, and a written WhatsApp summary sent immediately after the call. No upsell pressure during the call.
Do I need a laptop, internet, or any investment to start?
No investment required to start. A smartphone and a basic internet connection is sufficient. All tools recommended in the program are free. The first income methods are zero-cost to begin.
